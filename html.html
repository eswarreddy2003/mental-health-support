What Does This Test Help With?

This test is designed to help you better understand your mental health. By answering the questions, you can gain insights into your symptoms and identify areas where you might need support. Remember, this is not a substitute for professional advice, but it can be a helpful starting point.

About Our Platform

We provide a supportive space for anyone seeking mental health guidance. Our platform offers helpful resources for anxiety, depression, PTSD, stress, and more.

Our mission is to make mental health support accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or circumstances. We believe that everyone deserves compassionate care and understanding.

Anxiety Symptoms Questionnaire

1. Do you find it difficult to control your worries?

2. Do you experience sudden feelings of panic?

3. Do you avoid situations that make you anxious?

4. How often do you feel restless or on edge?

5. Do you have trouble sleeping due to anxiety?

Depression Symptoms Questionnaire

1. Have you lost interest in activities you used to enjoy?

2. Do you experience feelings of worthlessness?

3. How often do you feel fatigued or low on energy?

4. Do you have trouble concentrating or making decisions?

5. Do you have thoughts of self-harm or suicide?

PTSD Symptoms Questionnaire

1. Do you avoid places or things that remind you of trauma?

2. Do you feel easily startled?

3. Do you experience negative thoughts about yourself?

4. Do you have difficulty sleeping due to trauma-related memories?

5. Do you feel emotionally numb or detached from others?

Stress Symptoms Questionnaire

1. Do you feel irritable or angry when under pressure?

2. Do you experience muscle tension due to stress?

3. Do you have trouble sleeping due to stress?

4. Do you find it hard to relax?

5. Do you feel overwhelmed by your responsibilities?
